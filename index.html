Form 1 English quiz

Fill in the blank spaces in the sentences below by choosing the best word.

  1. Naomi always likes wearing _____ clothes.

    a) Loose b) Lose

  2. They fought tooth and _____ till they won.

    a) Mouth b) Nail

  3. His mother bought two _____ .

    a) Ox b) Oxen

  4. You should refrain _____ telling lies.

    a) With b) From

  5. _____ was Lwanda Magere?

    a) Whom b) Who

  6. I had arrived in Thika two months _____ .

    a) Before b) Ago

  7. Although _____ boys and girls came to her birthday party, she was not disappointed.

    a) A few b) Few

  8. The cat has hurt _____ tail.

    a) Its b) It's

  9. We remained _____ throughout the evening prep.

    a) Quiet b) Quite

  10. Our teacher gives good _____.

    a) Advice b) Advise
